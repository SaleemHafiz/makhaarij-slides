Makhaarij of Letters
Where Quran letters come from when we say them.
What is Makhraj?
Makhraj = Exit Point
It’s the place a sound comes from in your mouth or throat.
Why is Makharij Important?
It helps us say Quran letters correctly.
Wrong makhraj = wrong meaning.

Nose Sound (Khayshawm)
Sound Name:
Ghunna
Comes from the nose during ن and م with shaddah.
Ghunna Example
Word:
ثُمَّ
You hear the nose sound on مّ.
Empty Mouth Sound
Long vowels:
ا
و
ي
Come from the open mouth and chest.
